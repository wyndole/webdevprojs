My Top 5 Movies

Butch Cassidy and the Sundance Kid

Director: George Roy Hill
Release Year: 1969
Starring: Paul Newman, Robert Redford, Katharine Ross

Synopsis: Two Western outlaws are on the run from a posse after a string of train robberies. They head to Bolivia where they hope to find a fresh start, but their past catches up with them.

The Sting

Director: George Roy Hill
Release Year: 1973

Starring: Paul Newman, Robert Redford, Robert Shaw

Synopsis: In 1936, two professional grifters team up to con a mob boss out of a large sum of money. Their elaborate plan involves a fake betting parlor and a series of intricate scams.

Blue is the Warmest Color

Director: Abdellatif Kechiche
Release Year: 2013

Starring: Adèle Exarchopoulos, Léa Seydoux, Salim Kechiche

Synopsis: A young woman falls in love with a college student who is also her best friend. The film explores their relationship and the challenges they face as they navigate love and identity.
